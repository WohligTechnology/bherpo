manish m nisar(shah)

Team Owner

‘Team Borivali Star’ believes in providing ‘Talent Finder Profile’ to community people of all age. It helps them in discovering the hidden talent and nurturing it. KVO community has tremendous diversity of Knowledge, skill and talent. Most spend their life trying to repair flaws whilst their talent & strength lies dormant & neglected There are two assumptions that guide the beliefs of ‘Team Borivali Stars’:-

1) Each community people possess talents that are enduring & unique.

2) Each community person’s greatest room for growth is in the areas of his or her greatest strength.

These two assumptions are the foundation for everything ‘Team Borivali Stars’ do with and for their community people- how to select, measure, train and develop people who bring pride & laurels to community.

The keystone of high achievements and happiness is exercising one’s strengths. ‘Team Borivali Stars’ provides powerful and accurate way to find out what they are’.

‘Team Borivali Stars’ helps community people to create ability to build their life around the talents & strength they possess & connecting KVO people at all level.
