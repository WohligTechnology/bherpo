About us

We are Kutchhi Visa Oswal Jain Community. First we were Deravasi & Sthanakvasi. Nani Paksh & Moti Paksh, Divided by various creeds & partitions. Then an idea changed so strong it changed the concept from You & Me to "WE" That Idea of getting people together under one roof as ONE is BHERPO , which literally means “Sharing Togetherness”.

A one of a kind initiative started together by Two prime bodies of Kutchhi Visa Oswal Jain Community.

SHREE CUTCHI VISA OSWAL DERAVASI JAIN MAHAJAN Estd. In 1830 & SHREE KUTCHI VISA OSWAL STHANAKVASI JAIN MAHAJAN Estd. In 1873 Both The Mahajans are now hand in hand.

Insipiration

Shri Mulund Kutchhi Visa Oswal Samaj On completion of its 35th year organized Bherpo In 2013-14. Which was runaway hit & caused the KVO community in Mulund to come closer like never before.

A lot of people who did not know each other are now very good friends. Most important advantage of Bherpo is that it as a match maker amongst the community for eligible bachelors.

Both Mahajans SHREE CUTCHI VISA OSWAL DERAVASI JAIN MAHAJAN & SHREE KUTCHI VISA OSWAL STHANAKVASI JAIN MAHAJAN have decided to follow the noble idea of Shri Mulund KVO Samaj & Organise BHERPO acts on much grander & extravagant Level in 2015-2016.
