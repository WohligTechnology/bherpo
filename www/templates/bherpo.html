Rules & Regulations

Aquatics:

Tele match type water games in swimming pool.

Water levels of about 4 to 5 feets.

Open group for 8 years & above (male & female). Aquatics swimcap must.

Skating Relay:

Team of 4 Mixed doubles skating 50 mts each. Quads or Inline allowed.

Two Male & two Female mix team of each group.

Handminton:

Team of 4 + 3 Males group (14 - 20) playing lawn tennis using hands instead of raquets.

Bucket Ball

A team of 7 + 3 players trying to score. Similar to basket ball.

Two teams: 7 member male team & 7 member female team. Group 12 + (with 3 of 35 +).

Handball

Team of 5 + 5 Females only 35 + playing handball in a fenced ground.

Lagori

Team of 7 players above 50 years (3 Females + 4 Males), to play conventional Lagori.

Triatholon

For Males 08 - 12 and 13 - 16 = 100 mts running + 15 full situps + 100 mts cycling.

For Males 17 - 25 and 26 - 35 = 200 mts running + 25 full situps + 200 mts cycling.

For Males 35 above = 200 mts running + 20 full situps + 200 mts cycling.

For Females 08 - 12 and 13 - 16 = 50 mts running + 10 full situps + 50 mts cycling.

For Females 17 - 25 and 26 - 35 = 100 mts running + 15 full situps + 100 mts cycling.

For Females 35 above = 100 mts running + 15full situps + 100 mts cycling.

Bring your own cycle any number of gears allowed

Relay

Mix gender race of 100 mts each with 2 males and 2 females from each of the category (08 - 12) & (13 - 16) & (17 - 25) & (26 - 35) & (35 +)

3 Legged Race

Team of 2 Males each group (08 - 12) & (13 - 16)

Team of 2 Females each group (08 - 12) & (13 - 16)

4 Legged Race

Team of 3 Males each group (17 - 25) & (26 - 35 ) (35 above)

Team of 3 Females each group (17 - 25) & (26 - 35 ) (35 above)

Tug of war

Team of 15 strong people pulling the oponent team beyond the touch line.

15 Member Team each group Male (Below 16) & (16 +) and Female (Below 16) & (16 +)

Surprise Games

Different surprise games - Only for those who not participated in any other event of Bherpo

Quiz

Topics: GK, Current Affairs, Sports, Cartoon, Science, Geography, Movies, TV, Music, Invention, Discoveries, Mumbai, Kutch, India. 8 Member core team of 8 years & above. In the core Team 1 member of each age group as below compulsory. Also 7 member Teams of age groups 8 to 12, 13 to 18, 18to 25, 26 to 35, 35 to 50, 51& above In each team including core team at least 3 female & 3 male compulsory

Street Play

Maximum time limit is 12 mins. Limited props, no electronic gadgets, no recorded music. Live music allowed Maintain spirit of street play only to be performed on stage.

Dance

Maximum time limit is 12 mins.

Cycling

30 K.m. race of 6 member Team containing 1 female & 2 members above 35 years.

All members of the Team to be of age 16 Years & above.
